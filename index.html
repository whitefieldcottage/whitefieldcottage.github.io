WhiteField Cottage

~ Sky Above, Lake Below! ~

Whitefield Cottage is a charming and beautiful two-storey waterfront property, situated just minutes outside of Eganville, ON. Whitefield Cottage offers a bright and comfortable living space, with spectacular views overlooking Mink Lake. The spacious four bedrooms and two full bathrooms can accommodate up to 14 adults.

Whether to wake up to stunning sunrises over the tranquil lake, sip on favourite drinks while taking in the splendid views from the dock, explore the pleasant waters on kayak, paddle boat or by swimming, enjoy meals in the spacious kitchen or on the lower or upper decks of the cottage, or gaze at the starlit skies and enjoy scrumptious s’mores by the warm fire, great times and adventures awaits here!

Whitefield cottage, the perfect retreat for friends, family, and loved ones to gather, relax, and have a marvelous time.

Cottage is winterized and available all year around.

For more pictures, follow us on Instagram or Facebook: @whitefieldcottag
